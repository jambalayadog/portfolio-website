Built PvP and PvE maps taking them from concept to release, following [long established Gears of War level design process](https://youtu.be/73Pqsk74Jc0?si=sqw9bTWPN80s_gTa&t=957). I greyblocked maps first for macro and then for micro details, configured them for various multiplayer modes (spawners, cinematics, cams), and maintained integrity during art passes. I extensively playtested and iterated based on feedback, and may have even implemented an Easter Egg or two.  Check out [Village](https://youtu.be/8C6Qsu9ZjbY?t=15), a DLC map from Operation 8 that focuses on close quarters combat.
