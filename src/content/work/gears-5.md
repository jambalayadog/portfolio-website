Built PvP and PvE maps taking them from concept to release, following [long established Gears of War level design process](https://youtu.be/73Pqsk74Jc0?si=sqw9bTWPN80s_gTa&t=957).

I greyblocked maps first for macro and then for micro details, configured them for various multiplayer modes (spawners, cinematics, cams), and maintained gameplay integrity during art passes. Maps are extensively playtested and iterated uponbased on feedback, and I may have even implemented an Easter Egg...

Check out [Village](https://youtu.be/8C6Qsu9ZjbY?t=15), a DLC map from Operation 8 that allows close quarters combat to shine.
