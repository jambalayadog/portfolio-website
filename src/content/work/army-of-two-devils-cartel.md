Designed major level beats for Army of Two: Devil's Cartel including [Gas Station](https://youtu.be/_gnqeOhnEZk?si=wHdvDU-pkdLUo2aF&t=759), [Radar Station](https://youtu.be/_gnqeOhnEZk?si=9qhlVpeWUuKKkJM6&t=1104), and [Airplane Boneyard](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SL57b0JI_Go) encounters. Specialized in cooperative mechanics requiring synchronized player actions including coordinated traversal, tactical positioning, and split and shared objectives. I enjoyed the game's customization options, which inspired me to prioritize player expression in later game design and subsequent projects.
