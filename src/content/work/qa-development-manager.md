## Online + Compliance

Managed cross-platform QA testing teams responsible for Online and Compliance QA for numerous EA titles during the online gaming revolution. Oversaw test efforts across multiple titles and platforms, from PC to PS2 and PSP, and also to the launch of Xbox Online and Xbox 360.

During that time, my testers proved their metal on NFS Underground 2 + Most Wanted, EA Sports Online Titles: FIFA, Madden, Tiger Woods, NBA, NHL, NCAA, and shooters like Medal of Honor and Time Splitters (half my team was sent to UK for 2 months!). And, two of my testers met on the project and eventually got married!

## Submission Audit Team

Tasked to create and lead the Submission Audit Team to pre-test projects and identify critical and costly issues ahead of certification. For this, I built a team of specialized testers from the ranks of EA QA, analyzed EA's historical data on third-party and first-party quality certification, built a test plan, and generated a submission confidence report for each project.
