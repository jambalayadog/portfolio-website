I was lucky enough to represent EA and Skate 3 at our London EA Showcase event, where we shared the floor with EA-partner published games.  Over the course of two days and two evenings, I gave an [insane amount of interviews](https://youtu.be/WRO58-NyUS8), hands-on demos and tutorials, and helped the MC facilitate the Hall Of Meat tournament during evening media event which was one of many highlights of an amazing experience.  Skate showed me just how large EA's marketing department's budget is.
